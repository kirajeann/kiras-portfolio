UX/UI Designer / Artist / Marketing

I am a highly creative individual who prides themselves on incorporating art and empathy into all of the work that I do. I have recently discovered a passion for creating new and improved user experiences through design. Throughout my life, I have always loved to comprehend people's needs. With that being said, UX/UI design has come so naturally to me. I excel in user research and ideation, defining the problem, design, and prototyping.

The course provided me with valuable insights on blending my creative talents with my empathetic nature to build a career that enhances both aesthetics and usability. I excelled in various aspects, including user research, analysis and planning, design, prototyping, and testing. This experience has solidified my passion for UX/UI design, allowing me to pursue a career that fulfills my creative aspirations and makes the world more intuitive and visually engaging.
