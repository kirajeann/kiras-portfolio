Product design

The product design process in UX/UI design involves researching user needs, ideating and sketching concepts, creating prototypes, and iteratively testing and refining the design to ensure a seamless and engaging user experience.

Art direction

The art direction process in UX/UI design involves crafting the visual style and aesthetic guidelines to ensure a cohesive and engaging user experience across all digital platforms.

Visual design

The visual design process in UX/UI design involves creating aesthetically pleasing and cohesive interfaces that enhance user experience by strategically using elements like color, typography, imagery, and layout to guide and engage users.

I excelled in various aspects, including user research, analysis and planning, design, prototyping, and testing. This experience has solidified my passion for UX/UI design, allowing me to pursue a career that fulfills my creative aspirations and makes the world more intuitive and visually engaging.
